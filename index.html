Our Story

A Trusted Thai Plastic Supplier

Thaicharoen Plastic is a Thailand-based supplier of commodity plastics, built around a simple focus: consistent quality, fair pricing, and dependable delivery for the industries that rely on us.

We supply a focused range of commodity plastics — Polypropylene (PP), Polyethylene (PE), and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) — serving industries from packaging and agriculture to automotive and construction.

Our commitment to quality and on-time delivery is what earns us long-term partnerships with clients across Thailand. We maintain rigorous quality control to ensure every batch meets industry standards.
