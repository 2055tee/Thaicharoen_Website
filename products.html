PP

Polypropylene

Versatile, Lightweight & Chemical-Resistant

Polypropylene is one of the most widely used thermoplastics in the world. Its unique combination of stiffness, heat resistance, and chemical inertness makes it ideal for a huge range of applications — from food containers and automotive components to textiles and medical devices.

We offer PP in homopolymer and copolymer grades, in pellet form, suitable for injection moulding, extrusion, blow moulding, and thermoforming processes.

Key Properties

High stiffness & rigidity
Excellent chemical resistance
Fatigue resistance (living hinges)
Low density (0.90–0.91 g/cm³)
Melt point: 130–171°C
Food-safe grades available

Common Applications

Food Packaging Automotive Parts Pipes & Fittings Consumer Products Woven Bags Medical Devices

PE

Polyethylene (LLDPE)

Flexible, Tough & Puncture-Resistant

Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) is prized for its outstanding toughness, moisture resistance, and ease of processing. It provides excellent flexibility and puncture resistance, making it a top choice for stretch films and flexible packaging.

Looking for something more rigid? See our HDPE range below for bottles, pipes, and structural applications.

Key Properties

High puncture resistance
Excellent moisture barrier
Good stretch & cling
Easy to weld and fabricate
FDA-compliant grades
Recyclable (Code 4)

Common Applications

Agricultural Film Stretch Film Plastic Bags Flexible Packaging Liners
HDPE

High-Density Polyethylene

Rigid, Strong & Impact-Resistant

High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) stands out for its strength-to-density ratio, rigidity, and resistance to impact and chemicals. Its tightly packed molecular structure makes it the material of choice for bottles, pipes, and containers that need to hold their shape under stress.

HDPE is widely used in structural and packaging applications where strength and durability matter — from water and drainage pipes to industrial drums and heavy-duty containers.

Key Properties

High strength & rigidity
Excellent chemical resistance
Good impact resistance
Moisture-proof barrier
Density: 0.93–0.97 g/cm³
Recyclable (Code 2)

Common Applications

Water & Drainage Pipes Bottles & Tanks Containers & Drums Crates Caps & Closures
